insurer it sends a CONSOLIDATED 30-day summary (averages + risk band), never
the day-by-day rows. Both are shown below.

PART 3 — WHY THESE METRICS MOVE AN INSURANCE PRICE
------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Resting heart rate (better=lower): Strong cardiovascular mortality predictor; lower = lower risk.
• Blood pressure (better=lower): Hypertension is a major rating factor.
• Body mass index (better=range): Obesity correlates with diabetes & heart disease; >35 often substandard.
• VO2max (fitness) (better=higher): High VO2max is strongly cardioprotective.
• Daily activity (better=higher): Physical activity lowers all-cause mortality.
• Sleep duration (better=range): ~7h optimal; <5h sharply raises mortality risk.
• Heart-rate variability (better=higher): Autonomic health / stress resilience.

Note: values are realistic synthetic samples in the app's generator ranges.
On a real device, steps/sleep/vitals come from Apple HealthKit. The encryption
-> Walrus -> Canton-consent pipeline is identical either way.
